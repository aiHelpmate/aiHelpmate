### 🧠 About My Research Philosophy

> I'm a Data Science postgraduate at the **University of Liverpool**, where I navigate the frontier where **Reinforcement Learning** meets **Generative Models**.

My core conviction is that the next leap in AI isn't about scale—it's about **closure**. Offline RLHF (PPO/DPO) locks models into static datasets, yet true intelligent agents must learn in open-ended, interactive environments. 

I am currently exploring the **Stability-Plasticity dilemma** in Agentic RL: how can we fine-tune LLMs for tool-calling (Hermes/ReAct) and long-term planning, without catastrophically forgetting the foundational knowledge baked into pre-training? My work bridges `04-SFT` → `06-RL Alignment` → `10-Agentic Systems`, with the engineering harness (`09-Eval`, `03-Distributed`) to back it up.
